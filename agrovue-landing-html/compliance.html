Our Compliance Commitment

Meridian Africa is committed to maintaining the highest standards of regulatory compliance across all jurisdictions where we operate. We understand that farmers, governments, and financial institutions require assurance that our platform meets all applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

Global Standards: We adhere to international best practices and local regulations to ensure our agricultural intelligence platform serves stakeholders worldwide with confidence.

Agricultural Sector Compliance

As a platform serving the agricultural sector, we comply with specific agricultural regulations and standards:

Satellite Data Regulations

Compliance with international satellite data usage and distribution regulations

  • Copernicus Data Policy compliance
  • NASA Earth Data licensing
  • ESA data access agreements
  • Commercial satellite data licenses

Agricultural Data Standards

Adherence to agricultural data collection and reporting standards

  • FAO agricultural statistics standards
  • GEOGLAM crop monitoring protocols
  • National agricultural census compliance
  • Crop classification standards

Subsidy Verification

Compliance with government subsidy and support program requirements

  • Government verification protocols
  • Audit trail requirements
  • Anti-fraud measures
  • Reporting standards
