Introduction

Welcome to Meridian Sentinel. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our satellite-powered agricultural intelligence platform. We are committed to protecting the privacy of farmers, agricultural organizations, and all stakeholders in the agricultural ecosystem.

Our Commitment: As a platform serving farmers and agricultural communities, we understand the sensitivity of agricultural data and are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of data protection.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Farm location and size details
  • Crop types and agricultural practices
  • Payment and billing information
  • Communication preferences

Agricultural Data

Through our satellite-powered platform, we collect:

  • Field boundaries and geographic coordinates
  • Crop health indices and vegetation data
  • Soil moisture and weather patterns
  • Yield estimates and harvest predictions
  • Historical farming activity data

Technical Information

  • Device information and IP addresses
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Log files and analytics data

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

Provide satellite-powered insights, crop monitoring, and agricultural intelligence

Analytics & Improvement

Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform and develop new features

Communications

Send alerts, updates, and important agricultural advisories

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

With Your Consent

We share data with third parties when you explicitly authorize us to do so, such as sharing farm data with cooperatives or financial institutions for loan applications.

Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers who assist in platform operations, including cloud hosting, payment processing, and satellite data providers. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights and safety.

Important: We will never sell your personal or agricultural data to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data:

Encryption

End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest

Secure Infrastructure

Enterprise-grade cloud hosting with regular security audits

Access Controls

Role-based permissions and multi-factor authentication

Regular Backups

Automated backups to prevent data loss

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions

Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

Right to Object

Object to processing of your data for specific purposes

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at compliance@merdianafrica.io
