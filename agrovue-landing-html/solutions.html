PRIMARY FOCUS: GOVERNMENT AGRICULTURAL PROGRAMS

Transform Subsidy Programs with Satellite Verification

African governments invest Billions of dollars annually in agricultural subsidies, yet lose 20-30% to fraud. Meridian Sentinel enables you to verify 100% of beneficiaries at $1-2 per farm—98% cheaper than manual verification—ensuring every dollar reaches legitimate farmers.

  • Eliminate Ghost Farmers: Automatically detect zero-vegetation fields claiming to be active farms
  • Prevent Area Inflation: Verify actual farm sizes against declared areas with High-accuracy Validated models
  • Stop Crop Misreporting: Identify crop types to prevent subsidy fraud (e.g., declaring rice, growing maize)
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Track crop performance throughout the season for early intervention
  • Data-Driven Policy: Access continent-wide yield maps, crop distribution, and performance analytics
  • Rapid Deployment: 24-48 hour verification vs. 4-8 weeks manual process
